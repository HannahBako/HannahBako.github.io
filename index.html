Hannah K. Bako

Data Visualization, Creativity, Design, Human Computer Interaction

I'm a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the university of Maryland, College Park. I currently work with Dr. Zhicheng (Leo) Liu in the Human Data Interaction Group. My research interests lies at the intersection of data visualization, human-computer interaction and design, where I focus on discovering how to inspire and support creativity in visualization design. I'm particularly interested in understanding how people conceptualize and implement design ideas and using that knowledge to develop tools that empower people to bring their ideas to life. In the past, I focused on developing intuitive tools to support visualization programming and design in D3 with Dr. Leilani Battle. I also have an MSc in Software engineering from Stevens institute of Technology and a BSc in Computer Information Systems from Babcock University.

I'm on the academic job market this year. Please reach out to me about potential opportunities or collaborations.
